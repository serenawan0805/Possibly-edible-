Seafood dumpling soup

Estimated cooking time

20 Minute

Ingredients

  • Pollock fish roll
  • Dumpling
  • Cabbage
  • Dried seaweed
  • Egg

Procedure

  1. In a pot boil water. Put in the Pollock fish roll and dumplings to cook until they float.
  2. Slick the cabbage into shreds and put it in the pot to cook.
  3. Add in dried seaweed, soy sauce, and salt. Stir. Crack an egg into the soup.
  4. Lastly, top it off with sliced green onions!
Health benefit

Seaweed is the best dietary source of iodine, which helps support your thyroid gland. It also contains other vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin K, B vitamins, zinc, and iron, along with antioxidants that help protect your cells from damage.
