Shredded meat and veggies

Estimated cooking time

30 Minute

Ingredients

  • Dried wood ear
  • Carrot
  • Green pepper
  • Chicken thigh

Procedure

  1. Cut the chicken thigh into thin shreds. Put it in a bowl and add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and flour that make the meat tender. Let it sit for a bit.
  2. Soak the dried wood ear. Cut them into thin shreds once itâ€™s fully soaked.
  3. Wash the carrot and green pepper. Cut them into thin shreds.
  4. In a pan, heat the oil and stir the chicken thigh first until they change color. Add in the wood ear, green pepper, and carrot shreds. Stir.
  5. In a bowl, mix water, flour, soy sauce, oyster sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and flavoring of your liking. Add it into the pan and let it simmer on low heat.
  6. Lastly, top it off with sesame seeds!
Health benefit

Carrots are rich in nutrients that promote your health. They contain antioxidants, which may help protect your cells from damage and prevent conditions like cancer and heart disease.
