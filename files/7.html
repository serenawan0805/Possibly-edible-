Broccoli with Shrimp

Estimated cooking time

30 Minute

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Tomato

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, mix shrimp with cooking wine, black pepper, and sliced ginger to get rid of the fishiness. Let it sit for a while before boiling it in a pot of boiling water.
  2. Clean the broccoli well before cutting it into smaller pieces. Boil a pot of water and cook the broccoli until the color changes.
  3. Peel the skin off the tomato and slice it into small pieces.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and add in minced garlic. Put the sliced tomatoes in and stir it until it completely turns into liquid. Add in the shrimp and broccoli. Let it cook for a while before adding in the soy sauce, salt, and seasoning of your choice!
  5. Lastly, top it off with sesame seeds.
Health benefit

Shrimp is high in several vitamins and minerals, and it's a rich source of protein.
