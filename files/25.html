Choy sum with tofu soup

Estimated cooking time

20 Minute

Ingredients

  • Choy sum
  • Tofu
  • Dried seaweed
  • Clear noodles

Procedure

  1. Wash the choy sum and separate the leaves.
  2. Cut tofu into smaller cubes.
  3. In a pot with boiled water, add in clear noodles and cook until soft. Add in choy sum and tofu. Stir lightly. Add in dried seaweed, soy sauce, salt, and seasonings of preference. Simmer until ready to serve.
  4. Lastly, top it off with sliced green onions and sesame seeds!
Health benefit

Choy sum is an excellent source of calcium, iron, and vitamins A and C, which are antioxidants that can help boost the immune system and protect the body against cell damage.
