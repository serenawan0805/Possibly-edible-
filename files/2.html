Crab Roe Tofu

Estimated cooking time

40 Minute

Ingredients

  • Soft tofu
  • Crab stick
  • Cilantro
  • Crab mustard(can be replaced by salted goose egg yolk)

Procedure

  1. Cut the crab stick(s) into tiny specks.
  2. Cut the tofu into small cubes and boil it for a bit in medium heat water with salt.
  3. Heat the pan with oil and add in crab mustard. Stir it until it becomes fluffy with no crumbs left. Add in the finely cut crab stick(s) and stir until all the specks are covered in crab mustard. Add in the tofu and stir it lightly or the tofu will be crushed into tiny pieces.
  4. In a bowl, mix water, flour, and preferred flavoring. Stir them together and pour it into the pan. Lightly mix everything. Put the lid on and let it simmer a bit for the soup to become sticky.
  5. Lastly, you can top it off with cilantro!
Health benefit

Tofu contains all the essential amino acids your body needs and is rich in minerals and vitamins!
